Windows Desktop Application

Debtor Creditor Analysis Tool

Analyse authorised party-wise receivables and payables from Tally with ageing, overdue classification and clear Excel summaries.

Main functions

  • Read authorised debtor and creditor outstanding data from locally running Tally
  • Separate receivables and payables for clear review
  • Prepare party-wise outstanding summaries
  • Group balances into ageing and overdue buckets
  • Filter the analysis by the selected period or party data
  • Export analysis results to a local Excel report

Controlled local analysis

Balance verification: The user must verify the active Tally company, bill allocations, opening balances, ageing basis and party classification before relying on the analysis.

The tool provides management analysis based on the accounting data available in Tally. It does not independently confirm balances with customers or suppliers and does not replace ledger scrutiny.

How it works

A local workflow for understanding receivables and payables.

Open the correct Tally company
Keep the authorised company open and confirm that outstanding and bill-wise data are updated.
Select the analysis basis
Choose the required date, ageing basis and supported filters.
Review party-wise results
Inspect debtor, creditor, overdue and ageing totals for each party.
Export and verify
Save the analysis to Excel and reconcile important balances with Tally ledgers.
