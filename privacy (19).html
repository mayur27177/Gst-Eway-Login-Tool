Debtor Creditor Analysis Tool Privacy Policy

How the desktop application handles local outstanding data, analysis reports and licence information.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

This policy applies to the Debtor Creditor Analysis Tool desktop application provided by Samriddhi Associates.

Accounting data processed locally

The application may read party names, ledger balances, bill references, bill dates, due dates, outstanding amounts and other supported accounting fields from the authorised Tally company. This information is used only for the requested debtor-creditor analysis.

Generated analysis files

Ageing reports, outstanding summaries and Excel exports are created and stored locally in locations controlled by the user.

No customer or supplier contact

The application does not contact debtors or creditors, send balance confirmations, collect payments or access bank accounts.

Licence validation

Licence Key, registered contact details, installation or device identifier, licence status and validation timestamps may be transmitted securely to the Samriddhi Associates licence service. Party balances and outstanding details are not required for licence validation.

Storage and retention

Local reports, settings and temporary analysis data remain under the user’s control and can be removed through the application or operating system where available. Licence records may be retained for activation, support, fraud prevention and administration.

Sharing and sale

Samriddhi Associates does not sell debtor, creditor or Tally data. Data may be disclosed only when required by law or when voluntarily supplied by the user for support.

User responsibility

The user must secure local accounting data and verify party classification, bill allocations, due dates, balances and ageing results before relying on the analysis.
